The Collector is one of the three bosses encountered at the end of Act 2.


Pattern
Always starts with Spawn and always uses the Mega Debuff on turn 4. Can only have a maximum of 2 Torch Heads throughout the battle.

If one or more of the Torch Heads are dead, has a 25% chance of using Spawn, 45% chance of using Fireball and 30% chance of using Buff.

If both Torch Heads are alive, has a 70% chance of using Fireball and a 30% chance of using Buff.

Cannot use Buff twice in a row and cannot use Fireball three times in a row.
