# Strategic Reference Data

This document contains curated strategic knowledge for the LLM advisor. This supplements the database with expert-level insights.

## Card Tier Lists by Character

### Ironclad (RED)

**S-Tier (Always Take):**
- Offering - Best card in the game. +2 cards, +2 energy for 6 HP
- Feed - Essential for scaling max HP, especially at high ascension
- Demon Form - Win condition vs. long fights
- Corruption - Game-warping with Dead Branch or Dark Embrace
- Reaper - Massive healing with strength stacking

**A-Tier (Usually Take):**
- Immolate - Best AoE in the game
- Battle Trance - Excellent draw, downside rarely matters
- Shrug It Off - Premium block + draw
- Pommel Strike - Attack + draw is premium
- Flame Barrier - Excellent defense, especially vs. multi-hit
- Feel No Pain - Insane with exhaust deck

**B-Tier (Contextual):**
- Carnage - High damage ethereal
- Uppercut - Utility attack with great debuffs
- Spot Weakness - Strength engine vs. attacking enemies
- Disarm - Permanent debuff is strong
- Impervious - Huge block in emergencies

**Cards to Avoid:**
- Clash - Too restrictive
- Flex - Temporary strength rarely worth a card
- Iron Wave - Mediocre at everything
- Warcry - Usually not worth the exhaust

### Silent (GREEN)

**S-Tier:**
- Wraith Form - Win condition, take intangible and figure it out
- Catalyst - Doubles/triples poison is nuts
- After Image - Free block every card
- Adrenaline - Free draw + energy
- Corpse Explosion - AoE that trivializes hallway fights

**A-Tier:**
- Noxious Fumes - Passive poison scaling
- Footwork - Block scaling
- Leg Sweep - Block + Weak is premium
- Dash - Attack + block is efficient
- Blade Dance - Great with shiv synergies
- Bouncing Flask - Solid poison application
- Predator - Draw + damage, great early
- Backstab - Free damage, doesn't dilute deck

**Cards to Avoid:**
- Dagger Spray - Too expensive for the effect
- Riddle with Holes - Expensive, awkward
- Grand Finale - Too unreliable without specific deck

### Defect (BLUE)

**S-Tier:**
- Echo Form - Double every turn is broken
- Glacier - 2 frost + block is premium
- Defragment - Focus is the best scaling
- Biased Cognition - +4 focus wins games, manage the downside
- Electrodynamics - AoE lightning trivializes hallways

**A-Tier:**
- Coolheaded - Draw + frost, no downside
- Chill - Cheap, applies Vulnerable
- Cold Snap - Frost + damage, very efficient
- Capacitor - More orb slots = more power
- Loop - Extra orb procs
- Self Repair - Healing is rare for Defect
- Ball Lightning - Fast damage + lightning

**Cards to Avoid:**
- Hello World - Too slow, dilutes deck
- Claw - Only good in dedicated Claw deck
- Thunder Strike - Usually not enough lightning

### Watcher (PURPLE)

**S-Tier:**
- Blasphemy - Skip to infinite damage with setup
- Scrawl - Massive draw when needed
- Wish - Insane when upgraded (Plated Armor or Gold)
- Vault - Extra turn is broken
- Ragnarok - Massive damage with divinity/strength

**A-Tier:**
- Talk to the Hand - Block generation in Wrath
- Tantrum - Wrath entry + damage
- Empty Fist - Enters Calm, efficient
- Mental Fortress - Passive block per stance change
- Like Water - Healing in Calm
- Conclude - AoE + end turn safety
- Swivel - Efficient Wrath entry

**Cards to Avoid:**
- Pressure Points - Too slow
- Sands of Time - Not impactful enough
- Judgment - Too situational

## Boss Fight Strategies

### Act 1 Bosses

**Slime Boss** (140 HP, A2+: 150 HP)
- Splits at 50% HP into 2 smaller slimes
- Kill the split slimes same turn or they merge
- Key: Have 60+ damage ready for split turn
- Bring: AoE, high damage attacks
- Relics that help: Akabeko, Vajra, Hand Mirror

**The Guardian** (240 HP, A9+: 250 HP)
- Alternates Offensive/Defensive mode
- Mode Shift at 40 damage resets with block
- Sharp Hide: 3 damage when you play attacks in defensive mode
- Key: Big attacks > small attacks
- Bring: Heavy damage, not multi-hit
- Avoid: Playing many attacks in defensive mode

**Hexaghost** (250 HP, A9+: 264 HP)
- Turn 1: Activate (6x2 damage)
- Inferno: Burns cards, damage based on hand size
- Key: Play cards to reduce Inferno damage
- Bring: Consistent damage, okay to have bigger deck
- Timing: Big block for Activate, play out hand for Inferno

### Act 2 Bosses

**The Champ** (420 HP, A9+: 440 HP)
- Execute (below 50% HP): Massive damage after applying debuffs
- Limit Break doubles strength
- Key: Avoid having debuffs when Execute comes
- Plan: Either one-shot from 51%+ or have artifact/remove debuffs
- Watch: He gains strength over time

**Collector** (300 HP, A9+: 315 HP)
- Summons two minions (Torch Heads)
- Minions can be re-summoned
- Key: Consider ignoring minions, focus Collector
- AoE helps but isn't required
- Watch: Don't let minions attack too many turns

**Bronze Automaton** (300 HP, A9+: 320 HP)
- Has two Orbs that buff it
- Hyperbeam: 50 damage, -2 strength after
- Key: Kill Orbs first if struggling, else focus boss
- Bring: Scaling, this is a longer fight
- Watch: Block 50+ for Hyperbeam turns

### Act 3 Bosses

**Awakened One** (300 HP, A9+: 320 HP)
- Phase 1: Punishes Powers (+2 strength per Power played)
- Phase 2: Heals, starts at 0 strength
- Key: Avoid Powers in Phase 1, use all in Phase 2
- Exception: Must-have powers still worth it
- Bring: Attacks, Skills, save Powers for Phase 2

**Time Eater** (456 HP, A9+: 480 HP)
- After 12 cards played, heals and cleanses
- Punishes infinite combos and 0-cost decks
- Key: Make each card count, avoid spam
- Track: Count cards played each "rotation"
- Bring: High-impact cards, not many small cards

**Donu and Deca** (250/265 HP, A9+: 265/280 HP)
- Donu: Applies strength buff, hits hard
- Deca: Block and protect
- Key: Usually kill Donu first
- Deca strategy: Can ignore block if your damage is high
- Bring: Single target damage, not AoE focused

### Act 4 Boss

**Corrupt Heart** (800 HP, A20+: 850 HP)
- Beat of Death: Damage when you play cards
- Invincible: Caps damage per turn (200-300 based on ascension)
- Can only be damaged 15 times per turn (Invincible buff)
- Key: Need consistent 200+ damage per turn
- Need: Artifact for status cards, healing
- Watch: Beat of Death punishes large decks

## Relic Tier List

**S-Tier Relics:**
- Dead Branch (exhaust decks) - Free cards every exhaust
- Snecko Eye - High-cost decks become broken
- Corruption (if you have it) - With exhaust synergy
- Runic Pyramid - Hold your hand
- Pandora's Box - Transform Strikes = huge upgrade
- Energy relics (Fusion Hammer, Cursed Key, etc.) - 4th energy is massive

**A-Tier Relics:**
- Bag of Preparation - Extra draw turn 1
- Kunai - Attack 3x = +1 Dex (Silent especially)
- Shuriken - Attack 3x = +1 Str
- Ornamental Fan - Attack 3x = 4 block
- Mummified Hand - Powers cost 0 sometimes
- Ice Cream - Save energy between turns
- Pocketwatch - Draw if playing 3 cards
- Girya - +1 Str per rest (up to 3)

**Situational but Strong:**
- Velvet Choker (if deck is small) - 6 cards/turn is usually fine
- Sozu (if you don't need potions) - +1 energy is huge
- Runic Dome (if you know patterns) - +1 energy when learned
- Coffee Dripper (if HP is high) - +1 energy, no rest healing
- Philosopher's Stone (early acts) - +1 energy, enemies +1 str

## Energy Math

**Base Energy:** 3 per turn

**Energy Relics (Boss pool):**
- Astrolabe: +1 energy, transform 3 cards
- Black Star: +1 energy, 2x Elite relic drops
- Calling Bell: +1 energy, gain 3 relics + 3 curses
- Coffee Dripper: +1 energy, can't rest
- Cursed Key: +1 energy, gain curse on chest
- Ectoplasm: +1 energy, can't gain gold
- Fusion Hammer: +1 energy, can't Smith
- Philosopher's Stone: +1 energy, enemies +1 Str
- Runic Dome: +1 energy, can't see intent
- Runic Pyramid: Hold hand between turns (not energy)
- Sacred Bark: Double potions (not energy)
- Slavers Collar: +1 energy in Elite/Boss only
- Snecko Eye: +2 cards/turn, randomize costs
- Sozu: +1 energy, no potions
- Tiny House: +1 everything
- Velvet Choker: +1 energy, 6 cards/turn max

**Other Energy Sources:**
- Happy Flower: +1 energy every 3 turns
- Lantern: +1 energy turn 1
- Sundial: +2 energy every 3 Shuffles
- Art of War: +1 energy if no attacks last turn
- Ice Cream: Save unspent energy

## Damage Calculation

**Base Formula:**
`Final Damage = (Base + Strength) × Modifiers`

**Modifiers:**
- Vulnerable: 1.5x damage taken (1.75x with Paper Krane)
- Weak: 0.75x damage dealt (0.6x with Paper Krane)
- Wrath (Watcher): 2x damage dealt AND taken
- Divinity (Watcher): 3x damage dealt
- Pen Nib: 2x damage on attack (every 10 attacks)
- Double Tap: Play attack twice
- Necronomicon: First 2+ cost attack plays twice
- Akabeko: First attack each combat +8 damage

**Block Calculation:**
`Final Block = (Base + Dexterity) × Modifiers`

**Modifiers:**
- Frail: 0.75x block gained
- Footwork+: +3 Dexterity
- After Image: +1 block per card
- Ornamental Fan: +4 block per 3 attacks
